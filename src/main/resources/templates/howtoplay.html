HOW TO PLAY?

Hello there Mario!, have you forgotten how to play?.
Don't worry!, We will help you!.
As an arcade game the controls are really simple: -Use A and D to move your character to left and right. -Use W to shoot (As fast as you can!).
You can pick up items just by walking into them.
Shoot the enemies with your fireball and try to defeat them, they will get hurt if you hit them.
A good tip would be to never stop moving, the more you move, the harder will be to hit you.
The goal is to defeat the final boss (Bowser), and get the highest score. Show your friends who's the best in the leaderboard!.
