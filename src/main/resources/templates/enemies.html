ENEMIES

The different enemies that you will have to fight are:


  • -Koopa Troopas: This is the most common enemy, they fly around and have different types:

    • -Green Trooper: Has a low attack speed and needs 2 hits to die.

    • -Red Trooper: Has a low attack speed and needs 3 hits to die.

    • -Blue Trooper: Has a medium attack speed and needs 4 hits to die.

    • -Purple Trooper: Has a high attack speed and needs 5 hits to die.

    • -Black Trooper: Has a very high attack speed and needs 10 hits to die.

  • -Ghost: Rare enemy that spawns in the 3rd level. It spawns at a random location and it will move a bit trying to hit you to fade away later.

  • -Bowser: Really powerfull enemy, he has to get hit 100 times to go down. He has a barrier which blocks your attacks, he shoots waves of 3 shots, he orders a rocket to come down at you and he is fast, way faster if he gets under 25% health, that will make him to enrage and increase his movement speed and attack speed.
