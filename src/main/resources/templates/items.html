ITEMS

The available items in the game are:

Fire Flower: this item will make you to shoot 3 fireballs in a cone for 3 seconds!. The damage is the same for every fireball, but you will be able to clear big waves of enemies when you need it!.
-Ice Flower: this item will make your shots to follow your enemies, going for the one that is the nearest to your position. If the enemy dies while the shot is chasing him, the shot will wait until other enemy moves around.
-Mushroom: this item will make you big again if you were a little and inoffensive Mario, giving you the chance to get hit again. The mushroom will moving, so you just have to survive until it comes to you!.
